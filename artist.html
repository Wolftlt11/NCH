Spinning Dreams in Clay: Ratna Prajapati

Meet the Artist

Ratna Prajapati, a master potter from Bhaktapur, has dedicated his life to shaping clay into exquisite works of art. His journey began as a young apprentice learning from generations of skilled artisans.

Preserving Tradition

Through his intricate designs and dedication, Ratna keeps the centuries-old pottery tradition alive, blending traditional techniques with modern artistry.

The Process

From kneading the clay to firing the final piece, Ratna’s process is a blend of patience, skill, and deep cultural roots. Watch him in action below.

“Clay is not just soil; it is a storyteller of our heritage.” - Ratna Prajapati
